Our Technology

Our approach allows us to leverage highly nonlinear, correlated and noisy data, to solve the problem at hand, whether that is characterisation of an existing compound, optimisation of a current material, or discovery of new materials. Our software allows clients to be much more efficient in targeting of experiments, and therefore delivers a significant reduction in time-to-market.
